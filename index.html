Spotlight Dish: Crab Cakes

Crab Cake Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup celery, chopped
  • 1/4 cup sweet bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, torn
  • 2 small garlic cloves, roughly chopped
  • 5 ounces shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
  • pinch of Cayenne pepper, black pepper, and kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Put celery, onion, and garlic in the food processor until finely chopped.
  2. Transfer vegetables to large bowl. Melt butter in now-empty skillet over medium heat. Add chopped vegetables, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/8 teaspoon pepper; cook, stirring frequently, until vegetables are softened.
